Medical & Dental

Wiesbaden Army Health Clinic, TRICARE Overseas referrals through ATLAS, English-speaking specialists, emergency numbers. Dental is separate.

10 entries · across 4 phases

Before You Move 2

  • Emergency Numbers: Wiesbaden

    Save these numbers before you arrive. 112 is German fire/ambulance; 110 is Polizei. On-post emergencies use 117 (ambulance/fire) and 114 (police).

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

  • TRICARE Overseas: enrollment, referrals, the ATLAS

    Active Duty Family Members enroll in TRICARE Overseas Prime. Call International SOS to register, schedule your first PCM appointment at the Army Health Clinic, and expect specialty referrals to route through ATLAS to off-post German specialists.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

When You Arrive 2

  • Wiesbaden Army Health Clinic (ARHC)

    Primary care for enrolled Soldiers, DoD Civilians, and Family members. Clay Kaserne, Bldg 1040. Enroll first, then call the appointment line.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

  • Pharmacy workflow: ARHC pharmacy, German Apotheke, and OTC differences

    ARHC pharmacy fills most TRICARE prescriptions on-post at no cost. German Apotheken fill prescriptions off-post (most TRICARE-reimbursable). OTC in Germany is tighter: many US OTC drugs are pharmacist-only. Learn the dual system before you're sick.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-20

Getting Settled 1

  • Specialty referrals: how to get to a German specialist

    Your ARHC PCM refers through ATLAS; TRICARE Overseas approves; you book. 2-5 days for routine, same-day for urgent. English-speaking specialists are plentiful in Wiesbaden: ATLAS has a searchable directory.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

Life in Wiesbaden 5

  • Dental care for kids: ARHC clinic, DoDEA screenings, and off-post Zahnärzte

    ARHC Dental sees ADFM kids for routine cleanings when space allows. DoDEA runs annual screenings. Complex care (orthodontics, sedation, emergency) goes to a Wiesbaden pediatric Zahnarzt. TRICARE Dental Program (United Concordia) reimburses off-post costs at 50–100% by category.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-20

  • Specialty referrals: how TRICARE Overseas actually routes you

    ARHC PCM writes the referral; TRICARE Overseas (International SOS) authorizes and finds the network provider. You book the appointment. Often German specialist, sometimes LRMC. Translation available. 3–12 week timelines; escalate to case manager if stuck.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-20

  • Having a baby in Wiesbaden

    Pregnancy confirmed at ARHC → ATLAS referral to German OB → Hebamme (midwife) → HSK or Asklepios delivery. TRICARE covers most. English-speaking OBs plentiful. German maternity care differs from US pattern: but it's excellent.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

  • Mental health resources: on-post and off-post

    ARHC behavioral health for routine; ATLAS-referral German therapists for longer-term; chaplain for confidential pastoral care; Military Crisis Line (988) 24/7; SHARP for assault response. Don't wait until it's acute.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-17

  • Dental care: on-post for AD, off-post for everyone else

    Active Duty use the Wiesbaden Dental Clinic (DSN 590-1515). Dependents enroll in TRICARE Dental Program (TDP) and see off-post German dentists. TDP coverage is strong; German dentistry is excellent.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16
