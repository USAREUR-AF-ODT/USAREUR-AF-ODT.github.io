PCS-out: the last 60 days in Wiesbaden

Start 60 days out with HHG pickup, legal office for wills update, Abmeldung (de-registration) from Wiesbaden, and gym cancellations. Ship POV 6+ weeks ahead. Close German bank before your last day.

Last verified 2026-04-16 5 min read

What

Coming in was slow. Going out is fast; and every loose end triggers a bill, a penalty, or a mail-forwarding gap. This is the 60-day checklist to leave Wiesbaden clean.

60 days out

Orders confirmed

  • Confirm RDD (Report Date to Destination) and PCS pickup window
  • Update DEERS with future address (some dependents need a US address by 30 days out)
  • Schedule outprocessing briefing with your unit

Legal housekeeping

  • Update will at the Legal Office (if spouse beneficiaries change, kids grow, or assets shifted during tour)
  • POA review; expire old ones that won’t need updating; set up new ones for the transition period
  • SOFA card remains valid until orders end; no de-stamping process
  • File remaining VAT form returns, close out your NF-1 count

Financial

  • Stop automatic renewals on German contracts (gym, mobile, streaming, car insurance)
  • Arrange final LES processing; Finance briefing

HHG scheduling

  • Schedule pickup: HHG + UB + express (typically 3 separate days)
  • Request dates 4-6 weeks out from RDD; peak PCS season (May-August) slots fill fast
  • Pickup days: 3 × days you must be present

Utilities

  • Notify German utilities (gas, electric, water) of your move-out date
  • Request final meter reading appointment in the week before you leave
  • Settlement invoices (Jahresabrechnung) may arrive after you leave; set up mail forwarding

30 days out

POV

  • Ship via VPC Bremerhaven / Emden; 4-6 weeks transit means you ship earlier than you might think
  • Drop-off: 5-6 weeks before you want the car in your destination
  • Clean, drain fuel to ¼ tank, keep one set of keys

Utilities + bills

  • Rundfunkbeitrag: notify de-registration (send the form via web portal)
  • Mobile phone: use Sonderkündigungsrecht with PCS orders to cancel contract; 4+ weeks notice
  • Internet: 4-week notice typical; some ISPs require up to 3 months if auto-renewed

Legal; Abmeldung preparation

  • If you lived off-post: you must Abmelden (de-register) from Wiesbaden. Book appointment at Rathaus
  • Documents: passport, Anmeldebescheinigung (current), lease termination notice, final moving date
  • No fee

Bank accounts

  • US on-post bank: keep open (fine stateside)
  • German bank:
    • Decide if you’ll keep it (some banks allow; useful for future Euro travel)
    • If closing: request closure letter, return debit cards, confirm automatic payments are redirected or stopped
    • Watch for Rundfunkbeitrag, utility final bills; they’ll hit the account after you leave

School records

  • DoDEA: request transfer records for each child. SLO coordinates with receiving DoDEA or US school.
  • Immunization records + any IEP documents

Pet preparation

  • USDA health certificate for US re-entry: required, issued within 10 days of travel (same process, reversed)
  • German vet final check-up 2-3 weeks before flight
  • Pet flight: confirm routing with airline

14 days out

Legal final visits

  • Abmeldung: walk into Rathaus with documents; stamp completed in 10 min
  • Legal Office final check; any remaining paperwork
  • Motor vehicle de-registration through DES (if you’re keeping the USAREUR plate on a US-registered car at your next post, coordinate; if shipping it and the car is changing duty stations, the plate typically stays valid until expiry)

Household wrap-up

  • Return loaner items to ACS (lending closet)
  • Final apartment cleaning if off-post (German landlord standards are high; hire a cleaning service €200-€500 if you don’t want to do it yourself)
  • Final DPW housing inspection if on-post
  • Return all library books, MWR rentals

People goodbyes

  • Unit / command formal departure
  • School / CYS / Chapel community
  • Unit sponsor thank-you (they helped you in; thank them)

Last 7 days

Arrival at new duty station prep

  • Confirm Army Lodge or hotel at new location
  • Confirm sponsor at new location
  • Carry-on essentials for flight (meds, passports, orders, laptop, one full change of clothes)

Pet-prep

  • Final vet visit (get re-entry health certificate certified)
  • Pet carrier prep if flying in-cabin

Final LES / travel voucher

  • File PCS travel voucher within 2 weeks of arrival at new station (DTS online)

Day of departure

  • Drop off keys at on-post quarters office or landlord off-post
  • Final mail pickup at CMR
  • Clean AirBnB / hotel if temporary housing the last night
  • Take photos of every room before departure; documentation if there’s a damage dispute

What often goes wrong

Utility final bills hit your German bank after you close it

Solution: keep the German bank open for 2-3 months post-departure, or set up a forwarding address for paper bills, or use a US-based German-bank account.

Car doesn’t arrive at CONUS port in time for you

Solution: budget for rental car 2-4 weeks at destination. PCS travel allowance includes some rental reimbursement.

Contract cancellations didn’t process

Solution: German contract law can pursue you to your next address if you don’t properly cancel. Cancel every contract in writing with PCS orders attached, before you leave.

Kids’ school records don’t transfer quickly

Solution: hand-carry copies (paper or PDF) of report cards, transcripts, and IEPs. Hand-carry to the new school.

Cleaning deposit disputes off-post

Solution: Professional cleaning service + photo documentation of final state. Most disputes resolve in tenant’s favor with good evidence.

Gotchas

  • Peak PCS season (May-August); everything is harder. HHG slots full, Army Lodge at new station full, rental cars short. Start 90 days out during peak.
  • Don’t cancel TRICARE Overseas before re-arrival; coverage continues through the transition. Call International SOS to initiate transfer to new region.
  • Auto-pay on German accounts to German billers; some continue after you cancel service. Check bank statements monthly after PCS.
  • The 90-day post-PCS window matters for some claims; damage claims on HHG must be filed within 90 days of delivery; vehicle damage within 90 days of pickup.
  • German security deposit; landlord has up to 6 months to return (not uncommon). Track yours.
  • Don’t bring US candy / liquid restrictions; carry-on liquids at FRA apply. Checked bag weight restrictions apply.
  • Ensure dependents’ passports are valid for 6+ months past travel; many countries require this.

Source notes

Synthesized from the garrison in/out-processing pages and the Stadt Wiesbaden registration policies (which cover Abmeldung). PCS-out is a pattern every Soldier repeats; the specific provider schedules (utility cancellation timelines, mobile contract notice periods) change; verify with each provider before relying on a specific deadline.
