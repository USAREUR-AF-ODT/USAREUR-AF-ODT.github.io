Clay Kaserne Library: books, wifi, and community programs

MWR library on Clay Kaserne lends books, audiobooks, movies, and video games free to ID-card holders. Wifi, printing, and programs (book club, German conversation, preschool story time) run weekly. Digital library app works worldwide.

Last verified 2026-04-20 3 min read

What

The Wiesbaden MWR Library is one branch, Clay Kaserne only, but it punches above its weight. You get physical loans (books, audiobooks, DVDs, magazines, video games), free wifi and printing, research databases, and a steady calendar of free in-person programs. A library card is free and works at any DoW MWR library worldwide.

The physical branch is convenient; the digital library is where most patrons get sustained use. Your card unlocks ebooks, audiobooks, streaming film, language-learning software, and ~45 online databases from anywhere with internet. Useful for deployments, TDYs, and off-post evenings when driving to Clay isn’t worth it.

Where

Clay Kaserne; BLDG 1029. Near the main personnel-services cluster. Small footprint, quiet study tables, a children’s corner, a few public computers.

No Hainerberg or Aukamm branch. Off-post, the Stadtbibliothek Wiesbaden (Mauritiusplatz 3, Innenstadt) is the city library and has a sizable English-language section; a Stadtbibliothek card is cheap (a few euros a year) and gets you into a separate collection.

When

Hours are awkward; closed Friday and Saturday, open Sunday. Plan around that.

  • Mon-Thu 1000-1800
  • Fri closed
  • Sat closed
  • Sun 1000-1800
  • Closed on U.S. federal and German holidays (double-closures are common around Easter and Christmas)

Contact

Contact details in the frontmatter. Walk up to the front desk with your DoW ID to register; the card prints while you wait.

Online and deployment services

  • Digital library: army.dodmwrlibraries.org; ebooks via OverDrive/Libby, audiobooks, streaming video (Kanopy-style), Rosetta Stone and Mango for languages, Brainfuse for K-12 tutoring, Ancestry-in-library
  • Deployed support: paperback book kits and digital content shipped to APO addresses on request. Ask at the front desk or the remote and deployed page
  • Research databases: legal, medical, trade publications, newspapers from all fifty states

Programs

Typical weekly/monthly calendar (specific days rotate):

  • Preschool Story Time; early afternoon, weekday
  • Book Club; monthly
  • Wiesbaden Writers Club; weekly, aimed at adults working on fiction or memoir
  • Chess Club; weekly, kids and adults
  • Brick Builders (LEGO); weekly, elementary kids
  • Sit & Stitch; knitting/crochet circle
  • German Conversation Meetup; practice conversational German with native speakers
  • ASL Club; American Sign Language basics

Check the MWR library contact page or the garrison happenings calendar for the current schedule; programs pause during holidays and school breaks.

Gotchas

  • Friday and Saturday closed. Don’t drive to Clay on a Saturday expecting to grab a book.
  • Parking on Clay requires a base pass for the vehicle; walk-in friends/guests need an escort through the front gate.
  • Overdue fines are gentle but not zero; return on time or renew from the OverDrive app before the date flips.
  • Digital library login uses your MWR library barcode (not your CAC). First login is from the physical card you registered; save the barcode to your password manager.
  • Off-post use of the Stadtbibliothek requires an Anmeldung receipt for the discounted resident rate. Bring it when you register.
  • Video games are console-specific and the collection rotates slowly; don’t expect the newest releases.

Source notes

Hours and programs from the Wiesbaden MWR Libraries page (verified 2026-04-20). Digital library feature set from the DoD MWR Libraries portal. Program calendar shifts weekly; call the branch for the current schedule.

  • MWR & Recreation Wiesbaden Sports and Fitness Center: gym, classes, intramurals Clay Kaserne gym runs Mon–Fri 0500–2030 with cardio, free weights, group classes, and intramural sports coordination. Free for ID-card holders; guests allowed with escort. MWR after-hours access available at some garrisons: check enrollment at the desk.
  • MWR & Recreation Commissary, PX, and Shoppette: the on-post shopping economy Tax-free US brands at DeCA Commissary. PX (AAFES) for electronics, clothing, home goods, and restaurant food court. Shoppette is a convenience store and gas station rolled together. Hainerberg is the main complex.
  • MWR & Recreation Outdoor Recreation (ODR): Europe trips, gear rental, travel hub ODR Wiesbaden runs weekend bus trips across Europe and rents camping, biking, skiing, watersport, and party gear at MWR rates. Clay Kaserne location. Book trips 4–8 weeks out; popular weekends (Oktoberfest, Christmas markets) fill within hours.
  • MWR & Recreation Restaurants and food culture: how eating out actually works German restaurants run slower and more casually than US ones. Ask for a table, seat yourself at a Stammtisch-style pub, pay at the table with cash or card, and tip 5–10% by rounding up. Sunday closures apply to grocery stores but not restaurants.
