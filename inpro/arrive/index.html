Phase 02

When You Arrive

Your first two weeks in Wiesbaden: lodging, ID enrollment, housing office, finance office, vehicle paperwork.

Checklist Track your progress through 6 key tasks →

Housing 4

  • The first 72 hours

    A triage-ordered run sheet for the three days after you land. Skip the ones that don't apply, but do them in this order.

    Verified 2026-04-16

  • Wiesbaden Army Lodge: where most PCS families land

    Hainerberg TLF: Mississippi Strasse 45, BLDG 7890. Open 24/7, rates per diem or below, breakfast included, walk to PX/commissary/DoDEA schools. Book before arrival; 90-day TLA clock starts the day you check in. Reservations@army.mil or DSN 548-9700.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-20

  • Housing Services Office (HSO)

    Every accompanied arrival starts here for on-post quarters, off-post lease review, and TLA. Walk-ins Mon/Wed/Fri; appointments only Tue/Thu.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

  • Geographic bachelor: arriving without Family

    Unaccompanied arrivals skip OHA in most cases, land in the barracks or off-post lodging, and navigate a distinct timeline. Fast to set up, slow to feel settled. Use the time for travel, PT, German classes, and career moves Family-accompanied Soldiers can't.

    Verified 2026-04-17

ID & CAC 2

  • The first-month checklist

    Week 1: sign-in, HSO, Finance, ID. Week 2: housing, SOFA stamp, TRICARE, banking. Week 3: Anmeldung, car registration, first LES. Week 4: schools settled, social integration. Print this.

    Verified 2026-04-16

  • CAC, DBIDS, and the ID Card Center

    Your CAC comes with you from CONUS. Family members re-badge at the ID Card Center: CAC for teens 14+, DD-1173 for dependents under 14. DBIDS (installation access) is separate, automatic once enrolled.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

Unit Integration 3

  • Garrison directorates: who to call for what

    USAG Wiesbaden runs through ~15 directorates (DES for security, DPW for housing maintenance, DHR for personnel, DFMWR for family programs, Legal, SHARP, etc.). Garrison HQ at BLDG 3340 North Clay. This entry maps the problem to the office.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-20

  • USAG Wiesbaden tenant units: who's where

    V Corps HQ, 2nd Theater Signal Brigade, 66th Military Intelligence Brigade, 5th Signal Command, and a dozen more. Clay Kaserne is the main footprint; many tenants have outposts at Hainerberg and Mainz-Kastel.

    Verified 2026-04-16

  • Reporting for duty: your first week at the unit

    S1 handles in-processing, Operations owns the mission, Supply gears you up. Expect 3–5 days of admin before real work starts. Learn your sponsor chain, your ERB/SRP, and where to find the S1 lanyard-shaped office.

    Verified 2026-04-17

Family Services 2

  • Army Community Service (ACS): Hainerberg

    The one-stop support hub: spouse sponsorship, lending closet, relocation readiness, financial counseling, EFMP liaison, and more. Bldg 7790, Hainerberg.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

  • CYS: child care, SKIES, sports, teens

    Child & Youth Services runs on-post childcare (CDC, SKIES), school-age care, Youth Sports, and Middle School/Teen programs. Subsidized by income. Register via webtrac; book early: popular programs fill in weeks.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

Finance 3

  • Banking strategy: US military banks, Community Bank, and German Sparkasse

    Keep a US bank (USAA, Navy Federal, Andrews FCU) for DFAS pay and ATM access. Add Community Bank on-post for checks/cash. Consider a German bank (Sparkasse, Deutsche Bank, or Commerzbank) only if you need SEPA outflow or a landlord insists on IBAN. Dual is standard.

    Verified 2026-04-20

  • TLA: the math, the reality, the extension

    10 days initial, extendable in 10-day blocks to 60 max for accompanied arrivals. Lodging + per-diem meals. Document your housing search weekly through HSO or the clock stops.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

  • Your first LES in Germany: reading the paycheck

    Your first LES after PCS hits with OHA, COLA, TLA, and possibly a travel voucher: all USD, all untaxed. Expect confusion; expect 30–60 days for it to stabilize. File your travel voucher within 5 days of arrival.

    Verified 2026-04-16

Vehicle & POV 1

  • Vehicle Registration (DES / RMV)

    Every POV registered in Germany goes through DES Registration of Motor Vehicles. Bring orders, insurance, US title, and $45/year. Inspection required first.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

Medical & Dental 2

  • Wiesbaden Army Health Clinic (ARHC)

    Primary care for enrolled Soldiers, DoD Civilians, and Family members. Clay Kaserne, Bldg 1040. Enroll first, then call the appointment line.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

  • Pharmacy workflow: ARHC pharmacy, German Apotheke, and OTC differences

    ARHC pharmacy fills most TRICARE prescriptions on-post at no cost. German Apotheken fill prescriptions off-post (most TRICARE-reimbursable). OTC in Germany is tighter: many US OTC drugs are pharmacist-only. Learn the dual system before you're sick.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-20

MWR & Recreation 3

  • Wiesbaden Sports and Fitness Center: gym, classes, intramurals

    Clay Kaserne gym runs Mon–Fri 0500–2030 with cardio, free weights, group classes, and intramural sports coordination. Free for ID-card holders; guests allowed with escort. MWR after-hours access available at some garrisons: check enrollment at the desk.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-20

  • Commissary, PX, and Shoppette: the on-post shopping economy

    Tax-free US brands at DeCA Commissary. PX (AAFES) for electronics, clothing, home goods, and restaurant food court. Shoppette is a convenience store and gas station rolled together. Hainerberg is the main complex.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

  • Your first weekend in Wiesbaden: a lazy itinerary

    You just arrived. You're jetlagged. You don't need to conquer Europe yet. Saturday: sleep late, walk the Kurhaus, lunch in the Altstadt. Sunday: Biebrich castle park, Rhine river, German bakery. That's enough.

    Verified 2026-04-16

Pets 1

  • Wiesbaden Veterinary Treatment Facility (VTF)

    Primary care + preventive for pets of military personnel. Bldg 1038, Wurger St., Clay Kaserne. Tue/Thu 0800–1600, Wed 0800–1200. Emergency pet care goes to civilian Tierklinik: not the VTF.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

Legal 2

  • SOFA card: the paper that makes everything work

    NATO SOFA status exempts you from German income tax, vehicle tax, and import duties. The SOFA stamp (Certificate of Entry) goes in your passport at the Passport/Visa Office within 90 days of arrival.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16

  • Legal Office: wills, POA, notary, and divorce

    The on-post Legal Assistance Office handles wills, powers of attorney, notary, and legal counseling free for Active Duty + dependents. For non-military legal matters (German criminal defense, immigration), you need a German attorney.

    Verify first Verified 2026-04-16
